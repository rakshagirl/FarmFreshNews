About FreshFuture™

FreshFuture™

  1. FreshFuture™ uses a web crawler and CockroachDB to analyze global article news headlines, television, and channels. FreshFuture™ uses Google Cloud Sentiment Analysis to feature statistics on top positive and negative reviews for specific crops, updating real-time.
  2. In the future, we plan to track products related to farming, like trackors, scythes, and fertilizers. This would allow local farmers to track all their necessary products in one seamless platform.
